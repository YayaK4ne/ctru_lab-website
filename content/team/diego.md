### Bio

Hello! My name is Diego and I’m a dentist from Chile, who then turned ML researcher for healthcare! 

At the **CTruAI Lab**, I focus on bridging the gap between state-of-the-art AI models, such as Computer Vision and NLP, and the real needs in healthcare. My main research interest is automating the diagnosis of chronic, non-transmissible diseases for better prevention. 

I have an intersectional profile of three equal parts: clinician, machine learning engineer, and entrepreneurship. Let’s connect!

### Core Expertise & Focus
* **Computer Vision & NLP:** Deploying deep learning frameworks to process clinical text, notes, and medical imaging datasets cleanly.
* **Chronic Disease Automation:** Building predictive models aimed at early tracking, pattern recognition, and diagnostic classification of non-transmissible chronic illnesses.
* **Intersectional Pipeline:** Merging real-world dental clinical perspectives with production-level ML engineering workflows and scalable venture logistics.

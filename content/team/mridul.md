### Bio

Hi, my name is Mridul. I am a dentist by profession and a fourth-year PhD student at the **CTruAI Lab** within the Faculty of Dental Medicine and Oral Health Sciences at McGill University. 

My work sits at the intersection of artificial intelligence and oral health, where my primary research focuses on quantifying the uncertainty of Large Language Models (LLMs) in oral lesion diagnosis. Ultimately, I aim to build trustworthy diagnostic AI that clinicians can safely rely on at the chairside. 

Beyond my academic research, I am an avid reader and love to travel. If you share an interest in responsible AI, dental innovation, or just have a great book recommendation, I’d love to connect!

### Research Interests
* **Trustworthy AI:** Measuring uncertainty and validation metrics inside Large Language Models (LLMs) deployed for clinical decision assistance.
* **Dental Innovation:** Translating computer vision and conversational diagnostics from computational theory straight into real-world, chairside clinical workflows.

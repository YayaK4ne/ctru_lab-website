### Bio

My name is Anjana Suresh Ummanath, and I am a dentist from India currently pursuing an MSc in Dental Sciences (Non-Thesis) at McGill University.

As a Casual Research Assistant at the **CTruAI Lab**, I work at the intersection of artificial intelligence and healthcare, with a particular interest in uncertainty quantification, trustworthy AI, and large language models. I am fascinated by the challenges of developing AI systems that not only perform well but also communicate their limitations and support informed decision-making.

Coming from a clinical background, I thoroughly enjoy bridging perspectives from healthcare and computer science to build safer digital solutions for patient care.

### Research Interests
* **Uncertainty Quantification (UQ):** Developing methods that allow deep learning systems to explicitly state their confidence intervals when making critical clinical evaluations.
* **Trustworthy AI:** Designing transparent, human-centered data systems that support reliable clinical decision-making.
* **Large Language Models (LLMs):** Fine-tuning generative models to safely process medical data without hallucinations or bias gaps.

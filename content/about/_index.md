### Introduction
**CTru-AI (Collaborative & Trustworthy AI)** is an interdisciplinary research laboratory dedicated to advancing trustworthy artificial intelligence through the study of uncertainty. As AI systems become increasingly embedded in healthcare, research, and society, understanding the confidence and limitations of these systems is essential.

Our work focuses on developing methods that enable AI to better quantify, communicate, and reason under uncertainty, ultimately supporting more reliable and informed decision-making.

Our research spans machine learning, uncertainty quantification, explainable AI, large language models, and AI applications in healthcare and cancer research. By combining methodological innovation with real-world impact, we aim to develop AI systems that are not only accurate, but also transparent, dependable, and aligned with human needs.

> We believe trustworthy AI must be transparent to those it serves; not just accurate, but interpretable and honest about its limits. The name **CTru ("See Through") AI** reflects this conviction, along with our commitment to collaboration across disciplines, institutions, and communities.

---

### Our Mission
To advance the development of collaborative and trustworthy artificial intelligence by creating methods that understand, quantify, and communicate uncertainty, enabling AI systems that support responsible, transparent, and evidence-based decision-making.

---

### Our Vision
To shape a future in which AI systems are trusted not only for their capabilities, but also for their ability to recognize limitations, communicate uncertainty, and work effectively alongside people in addressing complex real-world challenges.

---

### What We Value

* **Collaboration** – We believe that the most impactful research emerges from diverse perspectives and interdisciplinary partnerships.
* **Trustworthiness** – We are committed duly to developing AI systems that are transparent, reliable, robust, and accountable.
* **Scientific Rigor** – We pursue research grounded in methodological excellence, reproducibility, and critical evaluation.
* **Real-World Impact** – We strive to translate advances in AI into meaningful benefits for healthcare, science, and society.
* **Responsible Innovation** – We recognize the opportunities and challenges of AI and are committed to developing technologies that serve people responsibly and ethically.
